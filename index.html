What Funngro Does

Funngro connects companies with motivated local teens for short-term projects. We make it easy to post project opportunities and mentor young talent, enriching the community while helping your business.

Benefits for Companies

  • Fresh ideas and perspectives from enthusiastic teens
  • Support community education and youth development
  • Expand your capacity with motivated helpers

Types of Work Teens Can Do

  • Digital marketing assistance (social media, design, content)
  • Basic data entry and market research
  • Light assembly or packaging tasks
  • Customer service and front-desk support

How It Works

1. Post a Project

Create an account and list the tasks you need help with.

2. Select Teens

Review applications or match with recommended candidates.

3. Collaborate and Learn

Work with your teen team, mentor them, and achieve project goals.
